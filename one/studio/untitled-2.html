Food Chemicals

This program helps monitor hazardous chemicals in school snacks, specifically substances added to food to enhance flavor, color, or shelf life. These substances can have negative health effects if used excessively or without proper oversight. Through this program, snack vendors and schools are encouraged to be more aware of the dangers of using unsafe chemicals and are urged to provide healthier and safer food options for students.
