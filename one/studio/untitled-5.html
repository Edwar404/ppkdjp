MD 07 Garden

MD 07 Garden is a green area developed at Menteng Dalam 07 Elementary School. This garden is designed to provide a learning experience closer to nature, combining elements of environmental education and sustainability. Through the MD 07 Garden, students are expected to gain a deeper understanding of the importance of maintaining ecosystem balance and how to optimally utilize limited space. In addition to serving as a learning facility, the garden also contributes to creating a greener and more inspiring school environment.

Hidrophonik

The hydroponic system at MD 07 Garden is a pioneering innovation that offers a soil-free farming method. Mustard greens are grown in a water medium enriched with nutrients, enabling faster and more efficient growth. This program is the result of a collaboration with BPSIP DKI Jakarta, providing students with the opportunity to learn modern farming methods that conserve space and water. By utilizing hydroponics, the school not only introduces innovative agricultural techniques but also supports the concept of environmentally friendly and sustainable farming.
