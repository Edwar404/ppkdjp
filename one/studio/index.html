ONEF Program

Organic Waste Management, Nutrition Grade, and Food Chemicals Program

The ONEF program supports SDG 4 (Quality Education), SDG 12 (Responsible Consumption and Production), and SDG 13 (Climate Action).

Visi

Enhancing the importance of food literacy culture and encouraging the community to actively participate in creating a cleaner and more responsible environment.

Misi

1. Raising Awareness :

Enhancing environmental awareness within elementary school communities.

2. Development of Educational Media :

Creating engaging and educational media to promote food literacy.

3. Behavioral Change :

Encouraging sustainable practices among canteen vendors, students, and teachers.

4. Community Engagement :

Fostering a sense of togetherness and collective responsibility toward the environment.
